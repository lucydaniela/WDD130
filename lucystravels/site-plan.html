Best Places for First Time Travelers

Depending on where you're coming from, traveling out of state or out of country for the first time can be very intimidating. Whether you’re wanting to go to the farthest reaches or Asia, or to our northern neighbors in Canada, traveling away from home doesn’t have to be a scary experience. -Honolulu, Hawaii -Boston, Massachusetts -Waterton, Canada -Paris, France -Rome, Italy -Beijing, China What do all of these places have in common? All are used to and geared towards visitors. English is the 3rd most spoken language in the world! Even if it’s not the native language, there are always people and signs around to help you navigate your new adventure.
